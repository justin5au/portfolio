Typornography.

Warning: Explicit Typographical Content


We've all seen how typography can be proper and Swiss.. brace yourself for the opposite.

Typornography is a zine that brings out the most lewd, explicit, and salacious side in the refined letterforms we normally see in grids and columns.

Featuring the beautiful typefaces of DIN and Garamond, Typornography presents their entire families, from A to Z, in full bleed cover spreads.

Throughout this project, I employed hand-made typography, scanner distortion, illustrator effects, projected typo-photos, image glitching, and various combinations of the aforementioned methods to distort the typeforms. Layed out in alphabetical order, this zine documents my experimentation in the most graphic, kitsch, and bizarre ways type can be viewed.
