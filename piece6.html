Berry Patch

An iPHone Game Concept


Berry Patch is a side-scrolling action game designed for handheld devices.

Set in the future wasteland of post-nuclear Earth, the friendly rodents of the forest have lived in peace in the only non-contaminated area left on Earth. Berries, which are energized crystals, are the only sustainable source of power available to the creatures of the forest. Knowing this, the evil cyborg-fox army has stolen all the forest crystals for their own military gains!

You play as Ginger bunny, the strongest fighter of the NinJin bunny village. Accompanied by Ginger's trusted companion, Dido, your goal is to take back all the Berries that the evil foxes stole! Beware of robots, monsters, and the dangerous terrain!
